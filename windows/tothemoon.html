To The Moon [2025]

Student Project - 3D Animation

 

To the Moon is an animated short following a boy and his dog. As they gaze into the starry night sky, they’re filled with wonder and creativity, leading them to build a cardboard rocket from scraps in the garage. As their imagination takes over, they take off on their adventure into the stars, and they journey to the moon, experiencing the mysterious beauty of space. Back in reality, they sit and watch a real rocket soar across the sky, serving as a quiet reminder of how powerful a child’s imagination can be, and just how far it can take them.

 

My Contributions:

  • Rocket Scene Design: Built and scripted the rocket assembly sequence using cardboard models and custom animations.
  • Character Model Setup: Assigned textures and correcting rigging for the main character and dog models, ensuring they were ready for animation.
  • Technical Animation: Wrote support scripts to handle object attachment, animation state triggers, and non-root motion character movement.
  • Scene Polish: Tweaked skybox, lighting, prop placement, and timing to improve visual consistency and flow.
